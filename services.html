Collision and Auto Body Repair

We provide the full range of collision repairs on domestic, foreign and unibody vehicles, including collision related mechanical repairs. Your car’s frame and body are a complex system of interconnected parts. Even minor collisions can affect your car in places that are far from the point of impact. Ultra high quality collision repairs require a complete, holistic approach that starts with an understanding of advanced metallurgy and welding. The L.K Auto gets to both the visible and invisible damage with a proven, step-by-step auto body repair process. It’s an unmatched combination of comprehensive training, extensive experience, up-to-date equipment and facilities that gets your vehicle back to OEM standards. After repairing your vehicle, we thoroughly test it to ensure full safety. On completion of all repairs and inspections, we perform a complimentary wash and vacuum plus we also rustproof all our repairs. we guarantee the repairs we make to your vehicle, against any defects of workmanship for as long as you own your vehicle.

Refinishing

We use colour matching equipment to restore your vehicle to its original luster. The paint we use is accurate due to computerized colour matching. It has superior durability, resistant to fading and meets or exceeds your car’s original paint standards. If the scratch is through the paint, the area is sanded to bare metal and the primer is applied to prevent rust. The damaged area is then painted and our top quality clear coat is applied. Quality clear coat finishes bring out the shine and enhance the colour of your vehicle. The higher the quality, the richer the colour and the glossier the finish. Dents are rebuilt and blended to match the OEM contour of the vehicle. Rust inhibitor is applied before a primer coat. Then surface paints are meticulously matched and seamlessly applied and protected with a tough, mirror-like clear coat finish.

Maintenance Services and Repairs

Our mission is to ensure our customers’ vehicles are reliable and safe for themselves and others they share the road which. Regular maintenance is crucial for the longevity of your vehicle. Paint Correction

Paint Correction is the process of removing imperfections on the paint surface. These imperfections can range from and are not limited to swirl marks, minor scratches, sun rings, oxidization, holograms and bird dropping etchings. At The LK Auto we pride ourselves in our 3 stage polish. The first step is to examine the condition of the paint so we can tell you which issues are treatable and what can be done. After careful examination we begin the treatment and cleaning of the paint we polish your paint three times with three different grades of compound with three different polishing pads to bring out that showroom finish. This is not a regular kind of detail, this is truly a work of art which requires a true passion to bring out.

Interior Detailing

A professional detail goes through a systematic process to achieve that “fresh of the lot” clean. This process involves the use of equipment such as steam cleaners and carpet cleaning machines along with professional grade carpet shampoos and leather conditioners to get your vehicle back to its glorious self.
